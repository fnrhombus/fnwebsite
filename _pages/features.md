AgencyHub brings every part of your agency operation into one powerful platform. Here's what's inside.

---

## Policy Management

Keep your entire book of business organized and accessible. AgencyHub gives you a single source of truth for every policy, across every carrier.

- **Unified policy dashboard** — View all active, pending, and expired policies in one place with powerful search and filtering.
- **Automated renewal tracking** — Get alerts 90, 60, and 30 days before renewals so nothing slips through the cracks.
- **Document management** — Attach declarations pages, endorsements, and correspondence directly to each policy record.
- **Multi-carrier quoting** — Submit quote requests to multiple carriers simultaneously and compare results side by side.
- **Activity journaling** — Every touchpoint is logged automatically, creating a complete audit trail for E&O protection.

---

## Claims Tracking

Follow every claim from first notice of loss through final resolution. Keep your clients informed and your agency protected.

- **FNOL intake forms** — Capture first notice of loss details with guided forms that ensure nothing is missed.
- **Status pipeline** — Visual pipeline shows every claim's current stage at a glance: reported, under review, adjusting, settled, closed.
- **Client notifications** — Automated email and SMS updates keep policyholders in the loop without manual follow-up.
- **Adjuster coordination** — Track adjuster assignments, inspection dates, and settlement offers in one timeline.
- **Reporting** — Identify trends in claim frequency, severity, and resolution time by carrier, line of business, or producer.

---

## Commission Engine

Stop wrestling with spreadsheets. AgencyHub's commission engine handles the complexity so your accounting team doesn't have to.

- **Statement reconciliation** — Import carrier commission statements and automatically match them against expected amounts.
- **Variance detection** — Flag discrepancies between expected and actual commissions before they become write-offs.
- **Producer splits** — Configure complex split arrangements by producer, line of business, or carrier, and calculate payouts automatically.
- **Override tracking** — Manage hierarchical override structures for agency principals and team leads.
- **Payout reports** — Generate producer compensation reports with full detail on every policy that contributed to their earnings.

---

## Client Portal

Give your clients 24/7 self-service access without adding to your team's workload.

- **Policy overview** — Clients can view their active policies, coverage summaries, and ID cards anytime.
- **Certificate requests** — Automated certificate of insurance generation for commercial clients who need them fast.
- **Claims filing** — Clients can submit first notice of loss directly through the portal, pre-populating your intake workflow.
- **Secure messaging** — Encrypted communication channel between clients and their assigned account manager.
- **Mobile-friendly** — Fully responsive design works on any device, no app download required.

---

## Reporting & Analytics

Turn your agency data into actionable insights with built-in dashboards and custom report builder.

- **Production dashboards** — Track new business, renewals, and retention rates in real time.
- **Revenue forecasting** — Project future commission income based on your current book and historical trends.
- **Carrier performance** — Compare carriers by premium volume, loss ratio, commission rates, and service quality.
- **Producer scorecards** — Measure individual and team performance against goals.
- **Custom reports** — Build your own reports with drag-and-drop fields and save them for recurring use.

---

## Integrations

AgencyHub connects to the tools and carriers you already use.

- **Carrier downloads** — Automated policy and commission data downloads from major carriers via IVANS and proprietary APIs.
- **Accounting sync** — Two-way integration with QuickBooks, Xero, and other popular accounting platforms.
- **Email integration** — Connect your agency email to automatically associate messages with the right client and policy records.
- **VOIP integration** — Click-to-call from any contact record with automatic activity logging for supported phone systems.
- **Open API** — Build custom integrations with our RESTful API and webhook support.
