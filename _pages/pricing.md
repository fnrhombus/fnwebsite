### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a long-term contract?**
No. All plans are month-to-month. Annual billing is available at a 15% discount.

**What happens after the free trial?**
Your trial converts to a paid subscription. If you choose not to continue, your data is exported and securely deleted within 30 days.

**Can I switch plans later?**
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

**Do you offer discounts for agency groups?**
Yes. Contact our sales team for volume pricing on multi-location or agency network deployments.

**Is my data secure?**
AgencyHub is SOC 2 Type II certified, with 256-bit encryption at rest and in transit. We undergo annual third-party security audits.
