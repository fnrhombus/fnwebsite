## Our Story

Fidelity National Software was founded in 2018 by a team of insurance industry veterans and software engineers who saw a gap in the market: the tools agencies depended on every day were built in the 1990s and hadn't meaningfully evolved since.

After spending years watching talented agents struggle with clunky interfaces, disconnected systems, and manual processes that should have been automated a decade ago, we decided to build something better.

AgencyHub launched in 2019 with a simple premise: agency management software should work the way agencies actually work — not the other way around. We started with policy management and have been expanding the platform ever since, guided by feedback from the agencies that use it every day.

## Our Mission

We believe independent insurance agencies are the backbone of the industry. They provide personalized service, local expertise, and trusted advice that direct carriers and insurtech startups can't replicate.

Our mission is to give those agencies the technology they need to compete and thrive in a digital world — without losing the personal touch that makes them great.

## Our Values

**Agency-first thinking**
Every feature we build starts with one question: does this make life easier for the people working in agencies every day?

**Transparency**
We publish our uptime stats, our roadmap, and our pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises, no vendor lock-in.

**Continuous improvement**
We ship updates every two weeks, driven by real feedback from our agency partners. Our public changelog keeps everyone informed.

**Security & trust**
Agencies trust us with their clients' most sensitive information. We take that responsibility seriously with SOC 2 certification, regular audits, and a security-first engineering culture.
